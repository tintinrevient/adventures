How to play?

In every round, every unit alive takes its turn, in a row. Your goal is to stay alive as long as possible.

Raider Hydra deals his power (first number) as damage to the player.

Healer Hydra heals the adjacent Hydras with his power.

Buffer Hydra heals the adjacent Hydras with his power, and also increases their power by his own power.

In your turn you can choose which Hydra you wish to attack. Attacking each type grants you bonus effects. Attacking Raider Hydra increases your power. Attacking Healer Hydra increases your health (second number). When you attack a Buffer Hydra both your power and health are being increased.

At the end of the round, there is a "round luck" effect which may help you or not.

Good luck!
